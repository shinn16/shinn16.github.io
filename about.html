About Patrick

From a very young age I have always loved to design and build things. Legos and Erector Sets were my favorite toys. In the fifth grade, a few friends and I wanted to design and build a model rocket that could go into space, though we never achieved our lofty goal, we very much enjoyed the process of designing the rocket and building small models of it.

That passion to design and build has only grown since then. I elected to go into the field of computer science because it is one place where creativity and passion can truly thrive. I am a massive supporter of open source software and the ideals that come along with it. I hope to one day create a company that boasts a Linux based operating system that has the ability to compete head to head with Microsoft and Apple.
