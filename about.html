About Patrick

First off, why the beach theme for the site? Well, if you know me, then you know I am a huge fan of Hawian Print shirts. I have a rather large collection of them, 17 to be exact. Almost every day of the week in warmer months I can be found wearing one. I am also a fan of just all things beach like, my apartment in decorated with a wide variety of beach themed items. Since my life is essentailly decorated like a beach, I desided it would be fitting if my website followed suite.

From a very young age I have always loved to design and build things. Legos and Erector Sets were my favorite toys. In the fifth grade, a few friends and I wanted to design and build a model rocket that could go into space, though we never achieved our lofty goal, we very much enjoyed the process of designing the rocket and building small models of it.

That passion to design and build has only grown since then. I elected to go into the field of computer science because it is one place where creativity and passion can truley thrive. I am a massive supporter of open source software and the ideals that come along with it. I hope to one day create a company that boasts a Linux based operating system that has the abilty to compete head to head with Microsoft and Apple.
